   The :doc:`CustomEquipment </reference/Equipment/CustomEquipment>` customEquipment1 has a :doc:`CustomPressureGaugeAttribute </reference/Customization/CustomPressureGaugeAttribute>` with :ref:`AttributeName <dexpi-Customization-CustomAttribute-AttributeName>` "DesignOutletPressure" and an (arbitrary) :ref:`AttributeURI <dexpi-Customization-CustomAttribute-AttributeURI>` "http://www.example.org/d9ef71ee3cc874"DOTANDSPACEHACKThe :ref:`Value <dexpi-Customization-CustomPressureGaugeAttribute-Value>` is 1.2 bar.
