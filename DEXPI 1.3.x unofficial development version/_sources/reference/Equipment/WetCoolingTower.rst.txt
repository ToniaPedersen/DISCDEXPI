===============
WetCoolingTower
===============

Overview
========

.. rubric:: Class

A :doc:`CoolingTower </reference/Equipment/CoolingTower>` that derives its primary cooling effect from the evaporation that takes place when air and water are brought into direct contact.

.. image:: WetCoolingTower.*


.. rubric:: Supertypes
