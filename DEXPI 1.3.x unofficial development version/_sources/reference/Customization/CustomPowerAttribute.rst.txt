   As :doc:`CustomAttributeOwner </reference/Customization/CustomAttributeOwner>` is abstract, we consider :doc:`CustomHeater </reference/Equipment/CustomHeater>` as an arbitrary concrete subclass.

   The :doc:`CustomHeater </reference/Equipment/CustomHeater>` customHeater1 has a :doc:`CustomPowerAttribute </reference/Customization/CustomPowerAttribute>` with :ref:`AttributeName <dexpi-Customization-CustomAttribute-AttributeName>` "DesignPower" and :ref:`AttributeURI <dexpi-Customization-CustomAttribute-AttributeURI>` "http://sandbox.dexpi.org/rdl/DesignPower"DOTANDSPACEHACKThe :ref:`Value <dexpi-Customization-CustomPowerAttribute-Value>` is 400 kW.

   .. admonition:: proteus-example

        <Equipment
            ID="customHeater1"
            ComponentClass="CustomHeater"

              Name="DesignPower"
              AttributeURI="http://sandbox.dexpi.org/rdl/DesignPower"

              Type="Power"

        </Equipment>
