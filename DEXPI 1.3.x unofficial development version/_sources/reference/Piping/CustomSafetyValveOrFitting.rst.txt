==========================
CustomSafetyValveOrFitting
==========================

Overview
========

.. rubric:: Class

A :ref:`custom <dexpi-concept-custom-sibling>` :doc:`SafetyValveOrFitting </reference/Piping/SafetyValveOrFitting>`, i.e., a :doc:`SafetyValveOrFitting </reference/Piping/SafetyValveOrFitting>` that is not covered by any of the other subclasses of :doc:`SafetyValveOrFitting </reference/Piping/SafetyValveOrFitting>` (:doc:`BreatherValve </reference/Piping/BreatherValve>`, :doc:`FlameArrestor </reference/Piping/FlameArrestor>`, :doc:`RuptureDisc </reference/Piping/RuptureDisc>`, :doc:`SpringLoadedAngleGlobeSafetyValve </reference/Piping/SpringLoadedAngleGlobeSafetyValve>`, or :doc:`SpringLoadedGlobeSafetyValve </reference/Piping/SpringLoadedGlobeSafetyValve>`\ ).
