=====================
CustomWasteGasEmitter
=====================

Overview
========

.. rubric:: Class

A :ref:`custom <dexpi-concept-custom-sibling>` :doc:`WasteGasEmitter </reference/Equipment/WasteGasEmitter>`, i.e., a :doc:`WasteGasEmitter </reference/Equipment/WasteGasEmitter>` that is not covered by any of the other subclasses of :doc:`WasteGasEmitter </reference/Equipment/WasteGasEmitter>` (:doc:`Chimney </reference/Equipment/Chimney>` or :doc:`Flare </reference/Equipment/Flare>`\ ).
