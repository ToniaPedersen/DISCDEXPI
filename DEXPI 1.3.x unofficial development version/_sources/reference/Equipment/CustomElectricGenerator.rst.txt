=======================
CustomElectricGenerator
=======================

Overview
========

.. rubric:: Class

A :ref:`custom <dexpi-concept-custom-sibling>` :doc:`ElectricGenerator </reference/Equipment/ElectricGenerator>`, i.e., an :doc:`ElectricGenerator </reference/Equipment/ElectricGenerator>` that is not covered by any of the other subclasses of :doc:`ElectricGenerator </reference/Equipment/ElectricGenerator>` (:doc:`AlternatingCurrentGenerator </reference/Equipment/AlternatingCurrentGenerator>` or :doc:`DirectCurrentGenerator </reference/Equipment/DirectCurrentGenerator>`\ ).
