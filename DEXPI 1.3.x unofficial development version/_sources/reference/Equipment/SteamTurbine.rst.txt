============
SteamTurbine
============

Overview
========

.. rubric:: Class

A turbine that is a heat engine in which energy of steam is transformed into kinetic energy by means of expansion through nozzles and the kinetic energy of the resulting jet is in turn converted into force doing work on rings of blading mounted on a rotating shaft (from `http://data.posccaesar.org/rdl/RDS416744 <http://data.posccaesar.org/rdl/RDS416744>`__).

.. image:: SteamTurbine.*
