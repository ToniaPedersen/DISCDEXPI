==================
CustomAgglomerator
==================

Overview
========

.. rubric:: Class

A :ref:`custom <dexpi-concept-custom-sibling>` :doc:`Agglomerator </reference/Equipment/Agglomerator>`, i.e., an :doc:`Agglomerator </reference/Equipment/Agglomerator>` that is not covered by any of the other subclasses of :doc:`Agglomerator </reference/Equipment/Agglomerator>` (:doc:`ReciprocatingPressureAgglomerator </reference/Equipment/ReciprocatingPressureAgglomerator>`, :doc:`RotatingGrowthAgglomerator </reference/Equipment/RotatingGrowthAgglomerator>`, or :doc:`RotatingPressureAgglomerator </reference/Equipment/RotatingPressureAgglomerator>`\ ).

.. image:: CustomAgglomerator.*


.. rubric:: Supertypes

* :doc:`Agglomerator </reference/Equipment/Agglomerator>`

* :doc:`CustomObject </reference/Customization/CustomObject>`
