===========
CustomMotor
===========

Overview
========

.. rubric:: Class

A :ref:`custom <dexpi-concept-custom-sibling>` :doc:`Motor </reference/Equipment/Motor>`, i.e., a :doc:`Motor </reference/Equipment/Motor>` that is not covered by any of the other subclasses of :doc:`Motor </reference/Equipment/Motor>` (:doc:`AlternatingCurrentMotor </reference/Equipment/AlternatingCurrentMotor>`, :doc:`CombustionEngine </reference/Equipment/CombustionEngine>`, or :doc:`DirectCurrentMotor </reference/Equipment/DirectCurrentMotor>`\ ).

.. image:: CustomMotor.*


.. rubric:: Supertypes

* :doc:`CustomObject </reference/Customization/CustomObject>`

* :doc:`Motor </reference/Equipment/Motor>`
