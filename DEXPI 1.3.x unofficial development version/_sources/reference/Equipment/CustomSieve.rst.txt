===========
CustomSieve
===========

Overview
========

.. rubric:: Class

A :ref:`custom <dexpi-concept-custom-sibling>` :doc:`Sieve </reference/Equipment/Sieve>`, i.e., a :doc:`Sieve </reference/Equipment/Sieve>` that is not covered by any of the other subclasses of :doc:`Sieve </reference/Equipment/Sieve>` (:doc:`RevolvingSieve </reference/Equipment/RevolvingSieve>`, :doc:`StationarySieve </reference/Equipment/StationarySieve>`, or :doc:`VibratingSieve </reference/Equipment/VibratingSieve>`\ ).

.. image:: CustomSieve.*


.. rubric:: Supertypes

* :doc:`CustomObject </reference/Customization/CustomObject>`

* :doc:`Sieve </reference/Equipment/Sieve>`
