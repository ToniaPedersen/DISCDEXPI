=============================
PositiveDisplacementFlowMeter
=============================

Overview
========

.. rubric:: Class

A flow meter that measures the volumetric flow rate of a liquid or gas by separating the flow stream into known volumes and counting them over time (from `http://data.posccaesar.org/rdl/RDS418094 <http://data.posccaesar.org/rdl/RDS418094>`__).

.. image:: PositiveDisplacementFlowMeter.*
