=============
CustomWeigher
=============

Overview
========

.. rubric:: Class

A :ref:`custom <dexpi-concept-custom-sibling>` :doc:`Weigher </reference/Equipment/Weigher>`, i.e., a :doc:`Weigher </reference/Equipment/Weigher>` that is not covered by any of the other subclasses of :doc:`Weigher </reference/Equipment/Weigher>` (:doc:`BatchWeigher </reference/Equipment/BatchWeigher>` or :doc:`ContinuousWeigher </reference/Equipment/ContinuousWeigher>`\ ).

.. image:: CustomWeigher.*
