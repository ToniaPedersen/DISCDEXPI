=============
CustomTurbine
=============

Overview
========

.. rubric:: Class

A :ref:`custom <dexpi-concept-custom-sibling>` :doc:`Turbine </reference/Equipment/Turbine>`, i.e., a :doc:`Turbine </reference/Equipment/Turbine>` that is not covered by any of the other subclasses of :doc:`Turbine </reference/Equipment/Turbine>` (:doc:`GasTurbine </reference/Equipment/GasTurbine>` or :doc:`SteamTurbine </reference/Equipment/SteamTurbine>`\ ).

.. image:: CustomTurbine.*
