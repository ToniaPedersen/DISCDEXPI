=====================
CustomPipingComponent
=====================

Overview
========

.. rubric:: Class

A :ref:`custom <dexpi-concept-custom-sibling>` :doc:`PipingComponent </reference/Piping/PipingComponent>`, i.e., a :doc:`PipingComponent </reference/Piping/PipingComponent>` that is not covered by any of the other subclasses of :doc:`PipingComponent </reference/Piping/PipingComponent>` (:doc:`CheckValve </reference/Piping/CheckValve>`, :doc:`InlinePrimaryElement </reference/Piping/InlinePrimaryElement>`, :doc:`OperatedValve </reference/Piping/OperatedValve>`, :doc:`PipeFitting </reference/Piping/PipeFitting>`, or :doc:`SafetyValveOrFitting </reference/Piping/SafetyValveOrFitting>`\ ).

.. image:: CustomPipingComponent.*
