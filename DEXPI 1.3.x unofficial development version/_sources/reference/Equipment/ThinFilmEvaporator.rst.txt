==================
ThinFilmEvaporator
==================

Overview
========

.. rubric:: Class

A :doc:`HeatExchanger </reference/Equipment/HeatExchanger>` and evaporator for the purification of temperature-sensitive products by evaporation, where a thin film of the liquid product on the inner side of a vertical evaporation pipe is generated by a rotating wiper system.

.. image:: ThinFilmEvaporator.*


.. rubric:: Supertypes

* :doc:`HeatExchanger </reference/Equipment/HeatExchanger>`
