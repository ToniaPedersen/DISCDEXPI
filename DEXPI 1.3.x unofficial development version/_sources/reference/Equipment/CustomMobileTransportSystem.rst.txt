===========================
CustomMobileTransportSystem
===========================

Overview
========

.. rubric:: Class

A :ref:`custom <dexpi-concept-custom-sibling>` :doc:`MobileTransportSystem </reference/Equipment/MobileTransportSystem>`, i.e., a :doc:`MobileTransportSystem </reference/Equipment/MobileTransportSystem>` that is not covered by any of the other subclasses of :doc:`MobileTransportSystem </reference/Equipment/MobileTransportSystem>` (:doc:`ForkliftTruck </reference/Equipment/ForkliftTruck>`, :doc:`RailWaggon </reference/Equipment/RailWaggon>`, :doc:`Ship </reference/Equipment/Ship>`, :doc:`TransportableContainer </reference/Equipment/TransportableContainer>`, or :doc:`Truck </reference/Equipment/Truck>`\ ).

.. image:: CustomMobileTransportSystem.*


.. rubric:: Supertypes

* :doc:`CustomObject </reference/Customization/CustomObject>`

* :doc:`MobileTransportSystem </reference/Equipment/MobileTransportSystem>`
