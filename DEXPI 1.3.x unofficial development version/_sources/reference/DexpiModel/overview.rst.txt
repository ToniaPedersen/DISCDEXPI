Overview
========

The :doc:`DexpiModel </reference/DexpiModel/index>` package contains two classes:

- :doc:`DexpiModel </reference/DexpiModel/DexpiModel>`: the root of the :ref:`composition hierarchy <dexpi-concept-composition-hierarchy>`,

- :doc:`ConceptualModel </reference/DexpiModel/ConceptualModel>`: a container for the conceptual content of a :doc:`DexpiModel </reference/DexpiModel/DexpiModel>`, i.e., engineering information independent from its graphical representation,

- :doc:`ConceptualObject </reference/DexpiModel/ConceptualObject>`: the abstract superclass of all classes used in a :doc:`ConceptualModel </reference/DexpiModel/ConceptualModel>`.
