===========
CustomDryer
===========

Overview
========

.. rubric:: Class

A :ref:`custom <dexpi-concept-custom-sibling>` :doc:`Dryer </reference/Equipment/Dryer>`, i.e., a :doc:`Dryer </reference/Equipment/Dryer>` that is not covered by any of the other subclasses of :doc:`Dryer </reference/Equipment/Dryer>` (:doc:`ConvectionDryer </reference/Equipment/ConvectionDryer>` or :doc:`HeatedSurfaceDryer </reference/Equipment/HeatedSurfaceDryer>`\ ).

.. image:: CustomDryer.*
