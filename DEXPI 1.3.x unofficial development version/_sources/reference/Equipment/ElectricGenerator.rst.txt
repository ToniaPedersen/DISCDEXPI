=================
ElectricGenerator
=================

Overview
========

.. rubric:: Class

An electric rotating machine that transforms non-electric energy into electric energy (from `http://data.posccaesar.org/rdl/RDS415709 <http://data.posccaesar.org/rdl/RDS415709>`__).

.. image:: ElectricGenerator.*
