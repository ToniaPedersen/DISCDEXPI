==================
ScrubbingSeparator
==================

Overview
========

.. rubric:: Class

A separator that is intended to clean gas by washing the gas flow with water or with another liquid entering at the top of the vessel.

.. image:: ScrubbingSeparator.*


.. rubric:: Supertypes

* :doc:`Separator </reference/Equipment/Separator>`

   The class is implemented as a :ref:`Proteus XML element with RDL reference <proteus-concept-xml-element-with-rdl>`.
