===================
CustomHeatExchanger
===================

Overview
========

.. rubric:: Class

A :ref:`custom <dexpi-concept-custom-sibling>` :doc:`HeatExchanger </reference/Equipment/HeatExchanger>`, i.e., a :doc:`HeatExchanger </reference/Equipment/HeatExchanger>` that is not covered by any of the other subclasses of :doc:`HeatExchanger </reference/Equipment/HeatExchanger>` (:doc:`AirCoolingSystem </reference/Equipment/AirCoolingSystem>`, :doc:`ElectricHeater </reference/Equipment/ElectricHeater>`, :doc:`PlateHeatExchanger </reference/Equipment/PlateHeatExchanger>`, :doc:`SpiralHeatExchanger </reference/Equipment/SpiralHeatExchanger>`, :doc:`ThinFilmEvaporator </reference/Equipment/ThinFilmEvaporator>`, or :doc:`TubularHeatExchanger </reference/Equipment/TubularHeatExchanger>`\ ).

.. image:: CustomHeatExchanger.*


.. rubric:: Supertypes

* :doc:`CustomObject </reference/Customization/CustomObject>`

* :doc:`HeatExchanger </reference/Equipment/HeatExchanger>`
