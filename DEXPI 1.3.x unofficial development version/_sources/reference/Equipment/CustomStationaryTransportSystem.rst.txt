===============================
CustomStationaryTransportSystem
===============================

Overview
========

.. rubric:: Class

A :ref:`custom <dexpi-concept-custom-sibling>` :doc:`StationaryTransportSystem </reference/Equipment/StationaryTransportSystem>`, i.e., a :doc:`StationaryTransportSystem </reference/Equipment/StationaryTransportSystem>` that is not covered by any of the other subclasses of :doc:`StationaryTransportSystem </reference/Equipment/StationaryTransportSystem>` (:doc:`Conveyor </reference/Equipment/Conveyor>`, :doc:`Lift </reference/Equipment/Lift>`, or :doc:`LoadingUnloadingSystem </reference/Equipment/LoadingUnloadingSystem>`\ ).
