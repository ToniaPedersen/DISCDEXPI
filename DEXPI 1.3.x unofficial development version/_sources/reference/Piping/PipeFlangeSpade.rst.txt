===============
PipeFlangeSpade
===============

Overview
========

.. rubric:: Class

A 'line blind' and an 'artefact' that is a circular plate with no central opening and holes to match mating flanged ends. It is also equipped with a handle (from `http://data.posccaesar.org/rdl/RDS472679 <http://data.posccaesar.org/rdl/RDS472679>`__).

.. image:: PipeFlangeSpade.*


.. rubric:: Supertypes

* :doc:`PipeFitting </reference/Piping/PipeFitting>`
