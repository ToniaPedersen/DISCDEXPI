================
CustomCentrifuge
================

Overview
========

.. rubric:: Class

A :ref:`custom <dexpi-concept-custom-sibling>` :doc:`Centrifuge </reference/Equipment/Centrifuge>`, i.e., a :doc:`Centrifuge </reference/Equipment/Centrifuge>` that is not covered by any of the other subclasses of :doc:`Centrifuge </reference/Equipment/Centrifuge>` (:doc:`FilteringCentrifuge </reference/Equipment/FilteringCentrifuge>` or :doc:`SedimentalCentrifuge </reference/Equipment/SedimentalCentrifuge>`\ ).

.. image:: CustomCentrifuge.*


.. rubric:: Supertypes

* :doc:`Centrifuge </reference/Equipment/Centrifuge>`

* :doc:`CustomObject </reference/Customization/CustomObject>`
