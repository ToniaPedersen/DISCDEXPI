===============
CustomSeparator
===============

Overview
========

.. rubric:: Class

A :ref:`custom <dexpi-concept-custom-sibling>` :doc:`Separator </reference/Equipment/Separator>`, i.e., a :doc:`Separator </reference/Equipment/Separator>` that is not covered by any of the other subclasses of :doc:`Separator </reference/Equipment/Separator>` (:doc:`ElectricalSeparator </reference/Equipment/ElectricalSeparator>`, :doc:`GravitationalSeparator </reference/Equipment/GravitationalSeparator>`, :doc:`MechanicalSeparator </reference/Equipment/MechanicalSeparator>`, or :doc:`ScrubbingSeparator </reference/Equipment/ScrubbingSeparator>`\ ).

.. image:: CustomSeparator.*


.. rubric:: Supertypes

* :doc:`CustomObject </reference/Customization/CustomObject>`

* :doc:`Separator </reference/Equipment/Separator>`
