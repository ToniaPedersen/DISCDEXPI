.. admonition:: example

   As :doc:`CustomAttributeOwner </reference/Customization/CustomAttributeOwner>` is abstract, we consider :doc:`WasteGasEmitter </reference/Equipment/WasteGasEmitter>` as an arbitrary concrete subclass.

   The :doc:`WasteGasEmitter </reference/Equipment/WasteGasEmitter>` wasteGasEmitter1 has a :doc:`CustomMassFlowRateAttribute </reference/Customization/CustomMassFlowRateAttribute>` with :ref:`AttributeName <dexpi-Customization-CustomAttribute-AttributeName>` "DesignMassFlowRate" and :ref:`AttributeURI <dexpi-Customization-CustomAttribute-AttributeURI>` "http://data.posccaesar.org/rdl/RDS14286182"DOTANDSPACEHACKThe :ref:`Value <dexpi-Customization-CustomMassFlowRateAttribute-Value>` is 3 kg/min.

   .. image:: /diagrams/diagram22.*


   .. admonition:: proteus-example
   
      
      .. code-block:: xml
      
        <Equipment
            ID="wasteGasEmitter1"
            ComponentClass="WasteGasEmitter"
            ComponentClassURI="http://sandbox.dexpi.org/rdl/WasteGasEmitter" DOTS123="DOTS123">
          ...
          <GenericAttributes Set="DexpiCustomAttributes" DOTS123="DOTS123">
            <GenericAttribute
              Name="DesignMassFlowRate"
              AttributeURI="http://data.posccaesar.org/rdl/RDS14286182"
              Format="double"
              Type="MassFlowRate"
              TypeURI="http://data.posccaesar.org/rdl/RDS380789"
              Value="3"
              Units="KilogramPerMinute"
              UnitsURI="http://data.posccaesar.org/rdl/RDS1350719" />
            ...
          </GenericAttributes>
          ...
        </Equipment>
