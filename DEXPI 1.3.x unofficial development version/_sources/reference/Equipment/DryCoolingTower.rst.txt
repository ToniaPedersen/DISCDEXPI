===============
DryCoolingTower
===============

Overview
========

.. rubric:: Class

A :doc:`CoolingTower </reference/Equipment/CoolingTower>` that is an indirect contact heat exchanger where, by full utilization of dry surface coil sections, no direct contact (and no evaporation) occurs between air and water; hence the water is cooled totally by sensible heat transfer (from `http://data.15926.org/rdl/RDS14072386 <http://data.15926.org/rdl/RDS14072386>`__).

.. image:: DryCoolingTower.*


.. rubric:: Supertypes
