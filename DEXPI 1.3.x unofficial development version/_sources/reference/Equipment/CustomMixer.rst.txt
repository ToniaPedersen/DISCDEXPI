===========
CustomMixer
===========

Overview
========

.. rubric:: Class

A :ref:`custom <dexpi-concept-custom-sibling>` :doc:`Mixer </reference/Equipment/Mixer>`, i.e., a :doc:`Mixer </reference/Equipment/Mixer>` that is not covered by any of the other subclasses of :doc:`Mixer </reference/Equipment/Mixer>` (:doc:`Kneader </reference/Equipment/Kneader>`, :doc:`RotaryMixer </reference/Equipment/RotaryMixer>`, or :doc:`StaticMixer </reference/Equipment/StaticMixer>`\ ).

.. image:: CustomMixer.*


.. rubric:: Supertypes

* :doc:`CustomObject </reference/Customization/CustomObject>`

* :doc:`Mixer </reference/Equipment/Mixer>`
